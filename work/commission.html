Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time?

+

Turnaround time varies depending on project complexity and current workload. Typically, simple projects like thumbnails may take 2-3 days, while more complex designs like advertisements or custom illustrations might take 1-2 weeks. Rush options are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes! All packages include revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Basic includes 2 rounds, Standard includes 3 rounds, and Premium includes unlimited revisions until you're happy with the result.

What file formats do you deliver?

+

Final deliverables typically include high-resolution JPG/PNG files. Standard and Premium packages include editable source files (PSD, AI, etc.) depending on the project. If you need specific formats, please mention this in your project description.

How do payments work?

+

For most projects, I require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining balance due upon completion before final files are delivered. For larger projects, we can arrange a custom payment schedule. I accept payments via PayPal, bank transfer, or other major payment platforms.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

+

Client satisfaction is my top priority. If you're not happy with the direction of the project, we can discuss revisions or adjustments to meet your expectations. In rare cases where we cannot reach a satisfactory outcome, I offer partial refunds determined on a case-by-case basis.

Do you offer commercial usage rights?

+

Yes, all commissioned works come with commercial usage rights for your business or personal brand. For extensive commercial distribution or merchandising, we may need to discuss additional licensing terms depending on the project scope.
