Hello, I'm Harshit!

A 21-year-old visual designer and digital artist from Haryana, India, passionate about bold aesthetics, purposeful design, and visual storytelling that makes people pause and feel.

Since 2019, I’ve been working as a freelance designer, helping brands, creators, and businesses shape their identity through high-impact visuals. My expertise spans graphic design, branding, UI/UX, and digital art, powered by tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, HTML/CSS, and cutting-edge AI platforms like Midjourney, Firefly, and ChatGPT.

In 2022, Ayush and I co-founded NYXDYLLON — a streetwear-focused clothing brand that fuses art, attitude, and culture into every thread. At NYXDYLLON, I lead the creative direction — turning concepts into visuals, visuals into identity, and identity into wearable expression. For me, design doesn’t stop on a screen — it lives on fabric, in motion, in moments.

I aim to blend clarity, creativity, and meaning in every piece of work.

I believe in staying fluid — adapting to trends without losing authenticity, and constantly experimenting with new ideas, tools, and formats to keep things fresh and human.

This site is my creative sandbox — a curated space to explore my design journey, ideas, and collaborations.
Let’s build something that resonates.
